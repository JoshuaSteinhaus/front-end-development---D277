About Carson City, The capital of Nevada

Carson City, the capital city of Nevada, boasts a population of approximately 58,130 residents. It was founded in 1858. Carson City holds the classification of an urban area. Carson.org The average income for Carson City is $37,261.00 and is on par with the rest of the state.

Quick Facts about Carson City

  1. Carson City named after the mountain man Kit Carson.
  2. Carson City is a cold semi-arid climate, with cold winters and hot summer.
  3. Carson City is at an elevation of 4,682 ft. and has an area of roughly 157.1 miles sq.
  4. Carson City is certified by (Better Cities for Pets)

About Nevada

Nevada's rich history dates back to Native American tribes like the the Northern and Southern Paiute, Washoe, and Western Shoshone who inhabited the region for thousands of years. Nevada has an exciting history full of interesting facts. It became the 36th state on October 31, 1864 Wikipedia. It produces about three quarters of all the gold mined in the United States. Many people claim to have seen extraterrestrials, and Nevada is home to Area 51.

Quick Facts about Nevada

  1. Nevada is the 7th largest state, roughly 110,572 square miles.
  2. The state flower is the SageBrush
  3. Nevada's state bird is the Mountain Bluebird
  4. Nevada is the fourth most seismically active state in the United States

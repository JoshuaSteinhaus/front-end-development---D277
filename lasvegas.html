About Las Vegas

Situated in the southern region of Nevada, Las Vegas is a bustling city with a population of approximately 656,274 residents. It was officially founded on May 15, 1905 and incorporated on March 16. 1911. With a suburban and rural mix, Las Vegas offers a exciting atmosphere for its residents and tourists alike.

Quick Facts about Las Vegas

  1. Las Vegas is named after hidden underground wells. The name Las Vegas means "the meadows".
  2. Las Vegas is the brightess place on earth and is easily visible from the International Space Station.
  3. People in Las Vegas eat more Shellfish in a single day than the rest of the United States consumes in an entire year.
  4. Las Vegas hosts over 300 weddings a day.
